### Usage

Typically used in [Footer](/using-spark/components/footer) to display company accolades and disclaimers.

### Guidelines

- Heading text can wrap
- Award images will stack by default and become side to side on larger viewports.
- Award images may be clickable links
- Follow [Toggle](/using-spark/components/toggle) usage guidelines.
